Chocolate Short Bread Cookies

Description

These chocolate bread cookies warenot only edible but adorable!

You can decorate them with sugar, melted chocolate or, if you feel a bit spazzy, wink wink, some royal icing! Fill 'em up with some frosting and caramel as well!

The possibilities are ENDLESS!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspooon instant espresso powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

Okay Michelins, time to get that bread.

  1. Sift together 2 cups plus 1 spoon of flour, cocoa powder, espresso powder and salt: set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter in a mixer until light and fluffy; beat in vanilla.
  3. Add dry ingredients in 3 seperate additions, mixing until just combined after each addition, until a thick dough forms.
  4. Flatten dough into a large disc and place in plastic wrap. Add to refrigerator for 30 minutes
  5. Line 2 large cookie sheets with parcment paper.
  6. Remove dough and roll out on a lightly floured surface to 1/4 or 1/2 inch thickness.
  7. Cut out shapes using shape cutters. Knead any dough scraps back together, re-roll, and cut out more cookies.
    8. Place cutouts 1 inch apart on the lined baking sheets and refrigerate for 20 minutes
  8. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  9. Bake cookies in the preheated oven. i baking sheet at a time, until cookies are just set, about 10 minutes. Allow cookies to cool n baking sheet a few minutes before removing to a wire rack to cook completely
