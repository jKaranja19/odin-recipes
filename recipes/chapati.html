Chapati Recipe

Description

Come get some of that Kenyan tasty vibes with our own Chapatis! A piece will leave you craving for more!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tabespoons of sugar
  • Vegetable or or margarine

Steps

  1. Add 3 cups of flour in a bowl. Pour in you tablespoons of salt and sugar into the flour and mix thoroughly.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of Vegetable oil or 1 spoon of margarine. Mix completely
  3. Slowly pour in the water in small amounts and knead the flour until the dough becomes non sticky. Add 2 - 3 tablespoons of oil and knead until the dough mixes well with it
  4. Leave the dough for 1 hour to allow it to mix with the oil. Take note: the longer you leave it out, the softer the dough becomes.
  5. Knead the dough again, then spread on a sufrace, then roll it out using a rolling pin. Divide the dough into strips of seven.
  6. Take each strip and roll them ino small balls.
  7. Sprinkle some flour on the surface and the dough and roll out the small balls into circles.
  8. Heat up your pan. After the pan is hot, place the circular dough on top and twirl it with your hand, DJ style
  9. Add a tablespoon of oil and ry each side until the chapati becomes golden brown
